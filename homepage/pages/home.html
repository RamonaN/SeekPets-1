ANNOUNCEMENTS

USEFUL TIPS

We know how hard it is to lose your favourite animal, so we want to keep things as simple as we can in order to help you find your most beloved one.

Here's a list of a few things that you can do in order to find your pet as fast as possible:

Update Your Pet’s Microchip

Often times pets come into the shelter with a microchip, but the contact information is not updated or the microchip is not registered. Make sure your information is updated by calling your pet’s microchip company, or using free microchip databases such as found.org.

File a Lost Pet Report

Call 311 to report your lost pet. The dispatcher will request your pet’s microchip number, your contact information and a description of your pet to create a Lost Pet Report in our database. This will ensure we have the right information on file in the chance your pet is found by an Animal Protection Officer or a member of the community.

Hang Posters & Flyers

Hang lost pet posters in your neighborhood, at local grooming shops, pet supply stores and veterinarian offices. You can also hang flyers at the Animal Services Center as well, just ask a Customer Service team member for more information.
